About us

We try to develop the
best cooperate feedback tool.

Our target is to develop the best possible corporate feedback tool and get a lot of happy users. Our product is great but we know we can make it much better. All revenue is invested in product development. Product first, business second.

Founder story

Half year ago I wrote our first pitch. Sent it to a lot of investors. Most investors didn’t reply anything. No one gave me the chance for a meeting. Was my product to weak, my pitch too bad or people just afraid because I look like a criminal. I was standing before the question, should I give it up? Hell no.

I invested my little savings and tried to learn to do many things by myself. Today standing here little proudly with my team presenting you our first version. Now we made it here and this is the beginning of a great new time. So I am just a beginner whose getting started. Very thankful for all kind of support and advise. Help me once - I help you twice.

About this company

We are now on the start of a great new journey.

Giving feedback

The new simple way
to give feedback.

Let your people give feedback with just a few clicks. Very easy, very quick and it’s make fun.

There are two types of feedback peer to peer feedback and team feedback.

Peer to peer feedback

One peer to peer feedback contains of three star ratings and in addition to that compliments or improvement ideas can be added. Giving feedback for example to five colleagues takes just around a minute.

Team feedback

With team feedbacks you collect your peoples opinion about how do they feel working in their teams and what do they think about their team leaders. Typically Team feedback is collected every second week.

Developing teams

A very efficient tool
for team leaders

Through these new feedback routines a lot of great data is created. With this data team leaders can easily follow how their teams and people are doing.

Follow your people

Team leaders can easily follow how their people are doing. Who is doing well and who maybe needs some support. For example: What do Peter’s colleagues think about Peter at the moment? How do they see Peter’s productivity, how his attitude and team working?

Follow your teams

Team leaders can easily follow how their teams are doing. In which teams do people feel happy and productive and in which teams people maybe don’t. How does the long term development of these teams look like and what could be the reason for it.

A lot of improvement ideas

Team leaders can easily collect their peoples opinions how their teams could be developed. What could we do better in this team? So you as a team leader will soon get a lot of improvement Ideas from your people - A great new idea source.

PDF Reports

Export all kinds of data to PDF documents

There are two types of feedback peer to peer feedback and team feedback.

PDF development reports

All feedback data can be exported to PDF reports. For example a summary report of Peters feedbacks development is a great attachment for the team leader for his next meeting with Peter. Reporting PDF reports is easy. Just select a team in the left bar then click the tab export reports. Select settings and click export.

How much feedback do your people on average get at the moment? In the average company people get rarely just a few feedbacks per month.

How to set up

Very easy to get started

Activating Team Feedback for your company is very simple and it doesn’t cost anything.

1. Register your company

Register your company and invite your people. You can enter your people’s emails manually or upload a csv file.

2. Setup your teams

Your people will receive an email to download the mobile app and to join their teams. Joining for your people is very simple.

3. Setup complete

Now you are ready to go. Your people can directly start give feedback and you can start optimizing your feedback routines.

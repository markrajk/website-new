Giving feedback

The new simple way to give feedback.

Let your people give feedback with just a few clicks. Very easy, very quick and it’s make fun.

Peer to peer feedback

One peer to peer feedback contains of three star ratings and in addition to that compliments or improvement ideas can be added. Giving feedback for example to five colleagues takes just around a minute.

Team feedback

With team feedbacks you collect your peoples opinion about how do they feel working in their teams and what do they think about their team leaders. Typically Team feedback is collected every second week.

Creating feedback routines

To increase the amount of given feedback team leaders can easily create feedback routines. For example that John will be requested to give Peter, Ella and Camille feedback twice a month. These feedback requests appear as a simple to-do list.

Receiving feedback

The new simple way to receive feedback.

Through these new feedback routines everyone will get much more feedback than at the moment. Everyone can now easily follow his own development.

Development charts

The vertical bars show the average of the latest feedbacks, so basically how people are doing at the moment. The horizontal bars describe the longterm average development.

Improvers and compliments

Everyone will get a lot of comments. Comments are divided in two categories: compliments and improvement ideas. What went well and how to improve. Comments can be answered by sliding them.

From rarely to regularly

At the moment in most companies people get feedback just rarely. Now everyone will get a small feedback weekly. So with just a quick look on the mobile, everyone can easily check how am I doing at the moment, what do my colleagues think about my contribution.

Developing teams

A very efficient tool for team leaders

Through these new feedback routines a lot of great data is created. With this data team leaders can easily follow how their teams and people are doing.

Follow your people

Team leaders can easily follow how their people are doing. Who is doing well and who maybe needs some support. For example: What do Peter’s colleagues think about Peter at the moment? How do they see Peter’s productivity, how his attitude and team working?

Follow your teams

Team leaders can easily follow how their teams are doing. In which teams do people feel happy and productive and in which teams people maybe don’t. How does the long term development of these teams look like and what could be the reason for it.

A lot of improvement ideas

Team leaders can easily collect their peoples opinions how their teams could be developed. What could we do better in this team? So you as a team leader will soon get a lot of improvement Ideas from your people - A great new idea source.

The dashboard

To get all of your data

All basic features can be done with the app. In addition to that there is the dashboard for advanced analytics.
